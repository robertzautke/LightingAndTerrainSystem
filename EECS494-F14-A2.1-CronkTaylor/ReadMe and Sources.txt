Welcome to Terrain Generator!
To bring the window up, click on Window on the top bar and then select Terrain Generator

Place a reference to your terrain into the terrian field, and then choose the algorithm you want
Once you choose an algorithm you are presented with parameters

Finally select "Generate!" to generate you terrain. 

There's also erosion algorithms to play with and a smoothing function.

If you mess up press "Undo Last Action!"
